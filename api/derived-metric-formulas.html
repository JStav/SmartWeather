Heat Index

Heat Index is calculated for temperatures at or above 80°F and a relative hummidity at or above 40%.

$$Index_{heat} = -42.379 + (2.04901523\times{}T) + (10.1433127\times{}rh) \\- (0.22475541\times{}T\times{}rh)-(6.83783\times{}10^{-3}\times{}T^2) \\-(5.481717\times{}10^{-2}\times{}rh^2)+(1.22874\times{}10^{-3}\times{}T^2\times{}rh) \\+(8.5282\times{}10^{-4}\times{}T\times{}rh^2)-(1.99\times{}10^{-6}\times{}T^2\times{}rh^2)$$

Source: Weather.gov

Wind Chill

Wind Chill is calculated for temperatures at or below 50°F and wind speeds above 5mph.

$$WindChill = 35.74 + (0.6215\times{} T) - \Big(35.75\times{}Wind_{sfc}^{0.16}\Big) + \Big(0.4275\times{}T\times{}Wind_{sfc}^{0.16}\Big)$$

\(T\) = temperature in degrees Fahrenheit (°F)

\(WindSpeed_{sfc}\) = wind speed in mph

Source: https://www.weather.gov/media/epz/wxcalc/windChill.pdf
